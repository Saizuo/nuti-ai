How To Buy

1. Create Wallet

Download Phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or Google Play for free. Desktop users, download the Google Chrome extension by going to Phantom.

2. GET SOME SOL

Have SOL in your wallet to switch to $Nuti. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on Phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

3. GO TO JUP.AG

Connect to Jupiter. Connect your wallet in chrome. Paste the $Nuti token address and confirm. When Phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

4. SWAP TO $Nuti

Switch SOL for $Nuti. We have Zero taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage.
